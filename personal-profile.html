Fan Yang

Principal Investigator

PI at Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Professor at Shenzhen Institute of Technology. Dr. Yang holds a Ph.D. in Molecular Genetics and Computational Biology from the University of Toronto and dual bachelor's degrees in Biological Sciences and Mathematics. She completed her postdoc training at Stanford University. Dr. Yang has published over 20 SCI papers, with more than 10 in top-tier journals (Nature, Science, Cell, Nature Medicine, Cell Host & Microbe), accumulating 5,700+ citations.
